Community Safety Guidelines & Liability Waiver

By joining the CARE Collective, I acknowledge and agree to the following:

Safety & Personal Responsibility
  • I am responsible for using good judgment when meeting or exchanging with other members.
  • I will meet in public places when possible and trust my instincts about personal safety.
  • I will communicate promptly if plans change or I am unable to follow through on an exchange.
Platform Limitations
  • The CARE Collective is a volunteer-based mutual aid platform and does not verify members' identities, backgrounds, or capabilities.
  • The CARE Collective and Missouri State University are not liable for any interactions, exchanges, or outcomes between members.
  • I understand that participation in the CARE Collective is voluntary and at my own risk.
Community Standards
  • I will treat all members with respect and avoid judgment or discrimination based on their situations, choices, or circumstances.
  • I will keep all shared information confidential and use member contact details only for CARE Collective exchanges.
  • I will report any concerns about safety or inappropriate behavior to the administrator.
  • I understand my membership may be paused or removed if my behavior compromises the safety or trust of others.
I have read and understand the above Safety Guidelines & Liability Waiver.
