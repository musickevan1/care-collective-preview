## üìñ Getting Started Guide

### Step 1: Log In
1. Go to https://care-collective-preview.vercel.app
2. Click "Sign In"
3. Enter your email and password
4. You'll be redirected to the dashboard

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile
1. Click on your profile (top right)
2. Add your name and location
3. Upload a profile picture (optional)
4. Save changes

### Step 3: Try Creating a Request
1. Go to "My Requests" from the menu
2. Click "Create Request"
3. Fill in the details (try different categories and urgency levels!)
4. Submit and see it appear in your list

### Step 4: Browse and Offer Help
1. Go to "Browse Requests" from the menu
2. Look through available requests
3. Click "Offer Help" on one that interests you
4. Start a conversation!

### Step 5: Explore Messaging
1. Check your notifications
2. Open a conversation
3. Send messages back and forth
4. Try the contact exchange feature (privacy-focused!)
